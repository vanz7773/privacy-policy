Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

School Management System (“the App”) is designed to help schools manage attendance, enrollment, academics, communication, and fee records. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information within the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Student name
  • Teacher name
  • Parent/guardian name
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Student ID or school-generated ID
  • Class and grade information

1.2 Academic and School Records

  • Student attendance records
  • Teacher attendance records
  • Assignments, submissions, and related information
  • Exams, grades, and academic reports
  • Enrollment data
  • Announcements and agenda data

1.3 Financial Records (Non-Sensitive)

  • Fee invoices
  • Payment history
  • Receipt records

1.4 Device Information

  • Location (GPS): Used only for teacher attendance and check-in verification.
  • Camera: Not collected (the app does not request camera access).
  • Contacts, photo library, microphone: Not collected.

1.5 Automatically Collected Data

We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics tools. The App may collect basic technical data such as device type, app version, and performance logs to improve functionality.

2. How We Use Information

  • Managing student and teacher attendance
  • Managing enrollment and class placement
  • Providing academic results and reports
  • Sending announcements and agendas
  • Tracking fee payments and receipts
  • Enhancing school–parent communication
  • Ensuring accurate teacher location (where GPS is used)

We do not use data for advertising or marketing.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share information only with:

  • The school administration using the App
  • Authorized teachers or staff
  • Service providers required for secure data storage or app hosting

All sharing is strictly for school operations only.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is used by schools and may collect information about students under 13. This information is provided only by the school or authorized personnel, not directly by children. We follow industry-standard protections to safeguard children’s data. If you believe a child’s data was collected improperly, contact us immediately.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data, including encryption, access controls, and secure storage. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as the school uses the App or as required by educational and legal standards. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data (subject to school policies). Requests should be made through the school administration.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date indicates the most recent change.
