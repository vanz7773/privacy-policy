Privacy Policy - My School App

Last updated: November 2025

My School App is a school management system designed to help schools manage attendance, enrollment, academics, fees, communication, and records. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information within the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Student, teacher, and parent/guardian names
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Student ID or school-generated ID
  • Class and grade information

1.2 Academic and School Records

  • Student attendance records
  • Teacher attendance records
  • Assignments and related information
  • Exams, grades, and academic reports
  • Enrollment data
  • Announcements and agenda data

1.3 Financial Records (Non-Sensitive)

  • Fee invoices
  • Payment history
  • Receipt records

1.4 Device Information

  • Location (GPS): Used only for teacher attendance verification.
  • Camera: Not collected
  • Contacts, photo library, microphone: Not collected

1.5 Automatically Collected Data

No advertising trackers or analytics tools are used. Basic technical data such as device type, app version, and performance logs may be collected to improve functionality.

2. How We Use Information

  • Managing student and teacher attendance
  • Managing enrollment and class placement
  • Providing academic results and reports
  • Sending announcements and agendas
  • Tracking fee payments and receipts
  • Enhancing schoolâ€“parent communication
  • Ensuring accurate teacher location where GPS is used

3. How We Share Information

Information is shared only with authorized school staff, administrators, or secure service providers required to operate the App. No personal information is sold or used for advertising.

4. Children's Privacy

The App may contain student data for users under 13, provided exclusively by schools or authorized staff. We do not collect information directly from children.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect all stored data.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as the school uses the App or as required by law, after which it is securely deleted.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their information through their school administration.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. The updated date will always be shown at the top.
